HTML이란 무엇인가?

HTML은 기본적인 언어이고 웹페이지 및 어플리케이션을 만드는 언어이다

In HTML, the element creates a line break. You can add it wherever you want text to end on the current line and resume on the next.


The HTML line break element can be used to display poems, song lyrics, or other forms of content in which the division of lines is significant.
